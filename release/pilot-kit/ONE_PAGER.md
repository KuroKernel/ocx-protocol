# OCX Protocol Pilot: Portable Compute Receipts

**Subject**: Pilot OCX: portable compute receipts, ready this week

## What It Is
Every job emits a cryptographically signed receipt (artifact/input/output/cycles). Anyone can verify offline.

## Why It Matters
Screenshots and logs aren't evidence. OCX receipts are.

- **Backup+restore** - Complete data protection procedures
- **Key rotation** - Secure key management and rotation
