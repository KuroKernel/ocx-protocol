# OCX Fairness Principles

## Core Commitments

1. **Proof, not surveillance** - Receipts are identity-free, offline-verifiable
2. **Own your exit** - Everything works self-hosted; our hosted services are optional
3. **Value-based pricing** - We charge for measurable time saved and disputes prevented
4. **Transparent pricing** - Public rate card, no penalties to leave
5. **Spec immutability** - v1 specification frozen forever; your receipts are portable

## Revenue Model

We earn money by providing convenience and assurance, never through lock-in:
- **Core protocol**: Free forever (MIT/Apache license)
- **Hosted services**: Optional APIs for scale ($5/M verifications)
- **Enterprise support**: SLA guarantees and custom integration help
- **Compliance services**: Audit trails and regulatory reporting tools

## User Rights

- Self-host any component without restriction
- Export all data in standard formats
- Verify receipts offline without network access
- Fork the protocol if governance fails
- Choose your own verification providers
